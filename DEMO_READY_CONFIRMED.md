## üö® COMMON DEMO QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: How is this different from LinkedIn?**  
A: LinkedIn is global. ZALPHA is Pacific Islands only, with education verification, skills testing, and school revenue sharing. Our students get preferential treatment because they're locally verified.

**Q: What if students lie about their skills?**  
A: All students take our gamified Basic Skills Assessment. Employers see verified scores before interviewing. We also verify student IDs through Plaid integration.

**Q: How do you make money?**  
A: Employer subscriptions ($149-$499/month), school revenue sharing (5% of placements), contract marketplace fees, and premium student features. Free plan available for contract-only work.

**Q: Why should schools partner with you?**  
A: Schools earn 5% on every job placement with zero cost or risk. We help them track graduate employment outcomes and provide FERPA-compliant student data access. Plus, we prevent brain drain by keeping talent local.

**Q: What about student privacy?**  
A: Students have FULL control. They can hide their profile from specific employers or even their own school. All interactions stay on-platform. FERPA compliant. They control what education data we can access.

**Q: How do you prevent abuse?**  
A: Section 7A of Terms of Service covers student account integrity. Automatic suspension for cheating, fake reviews, or credential fraud. Employers must report terminations within 30 days. Manual review for disputes.

**Q: Is crypto required?**  
A: No! Completely optional. Traditional payment methods available. Crypto wallet only for students who want to earn/cash out in cryptocurrency for gig work.

**Q: What happens if an employer doesn't report terminations?**  
A: Automated reminders at 20, 25, and 30 days. Account suspension after 45 days non-compliance. Manual review can restore access with verification.
