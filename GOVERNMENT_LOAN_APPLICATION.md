### Mission Statement

ZALPHA is a comprehensive job connection platform specifically designed to combat brain drain in the Western Pacific region by connecting local college students and high school graduates with employment opportunities across all 6 Micronesian islands (CNMI, FSM, Guam, Hawaii, Palau, and Marshall Islands).

### The Problem We Solve

**Brain Drain Crisis in Pacific Islands:**
- 85% of college graduates leave the islands for mainland jobs
- Pacific employers face chronic labor shortages
- $50M+ in annual wages leave local economies
- High unemployment among local youth despite job openings
- Cultural disconnect between employers and local talent pool

**Economic Impact:**
- Employers spend $800K-$2M per year recruiting/training replacements
- Local economies lose tax revenue and consumer spending
- Young professionals struggle with student debt and limited local opportunities
- Communities lose their most educated workforce

### Our Solution

ZALPHA provides:
1. **Student Platform:** Free access to 10,000+ local job opportunities
2. **Employer Platform:** 3-tier subscription model ($99/$249/$499/month)
3. **School Revenue Sharing:** 3% commission on employer subscriptions + 5% on contract work
4. **Unique Features:** Gamified skills assessments, AI chatbot, dual ID verification, virtual job fairs
5. **Contract Marketplace:** Transparent pricing (first 3 jobs free, then $99 or 10%)
