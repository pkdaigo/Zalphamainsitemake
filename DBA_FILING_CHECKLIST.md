## üö® TROUBLESHOOTING

### **Problem: DBA name "KiEX" is already taken**
**Solution:**
- Try: "KiEX Platform"
- Try: "KiEX Jobs"
- Try: "KiEX Connect"
- Try: "KiEX Careers"

### **Problem: Bank won't accept DBA**
**Solution:**
- Bring certified copy (not regular copy)
- Ask to speak with manager
- Show them this is a legal DBA filed with CNMI
- If they still refuse, consider switching banks

### **Problem: USPTO rejects trademark**
**Solution:**
- Read the office action carefully
- Respond within 6 months deadline
- Hire attorney to help respond (worth it!)
- Don't abandon application

### **Problem: Can't afford attorney**
**Solution:**
- File trademark DIY (save $1,500)
- Use LegalZoom for Terms/Privacy review ($500-$1,000)
- Ask attorney for payment plan
- Consider local CNMI legal aid services
