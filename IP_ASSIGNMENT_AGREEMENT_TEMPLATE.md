## 1. ASSIGNMENT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 1.1 Work Product
The Assignor agrees that all work product, inventions, designs, code, documentation, ideas, improvements, and other intellectual property created, developed, or contributed to the KiEX platform during the course of their work for the Company (collectively, "Work Product") shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

### 1.2 Ownership
The Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all right, title, and interest in and to the Work Product, including but not limited to:
- All copyrights and copyright registrations
- All patents and patent applications
- All trademarks and service marks
- All trade secrets and confidential information
- All source code, object code, and documentation
- All moral rights and attribution rights

### 1.3 Work Made for Hire
To the extent permitted by law, all Work Product shall be considered "work made for hire" under U.S. copyright law, with the Company as the author and owner.
